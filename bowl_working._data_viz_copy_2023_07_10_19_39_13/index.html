Food Prices Before and After COVID-19

This data visualization shows the changes in food prices before and after the COVID-19 pandemic in different countries around the world. The data is based on a random selection from a CSV file containing information about food prices.

The noodle bowl in the center represents the commodity being visualized, and the randomly placed noodles inside the bowl represent the change in price. Thick and wavy noodles represent an increase in price, while thin and straight noodles represent a decrease in price.

The percentage change in price is displayed at the bottom of the visualization, and the background color changes based on whether the price has increased, decreased, or remained the same.

To view a different commodity, simply refresh the page.
